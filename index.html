At 58, She "Balances" Her Type 2 Diabetes. This is her story...

Today, we talk to Tiwa, a 58-year-old woman who reversed her diabetes. She tells us how...

"Hello, this is Tiwa,

In 2009, shortly after my husband's death, I got diagnosed with type 2 diabetes.

When I checked in for a test, the doctor said my blood sugar is 265mg/dL.

From what he said, taking a few medicines he was going to prescribe will help me delay the severity of the disease

Because it's a long term disease that just never goes.

Although I was heartbroken, the deed was done and I had to do what the doctor told me to live a near-normal life.

Right there, on that spot, he brought out his pen and jotted down, in his poor handwriting, a list of drugs I should get in a pharmacy as soon as possible.

He also advised that I stop eating some foods totally and exercise my body a lot more often.

I never knew that day was what would start a long journey of horror in my life.

What started as a simple list of drugs kept increasing until I was spending over ₦120,000 every month to handle my type 2 diabetes.

I would visit a doctor at least 4 times every month and the one thing they recommended was that I stop eating carbohydrates, exercise harder and keep taking my drugs.

I was buying everything they recommended.

Yet, my condition wasn't improving.

I was piling on body fat around my belly and waist.

I couldn't do simple house chores without breathing heavily like someone climbing a mountain.

I was getting further and further apart from the woman I was while my husband was still alive.

And I felt exhausted every time.

My children had to help me with everything.

They're my only motivation to keep moving.

I didn't want someone else to take my motherly responsibility on them.

I was really, really getting worse everyday.

After trying out everything and almost giving up, a very good friend sent me a video. She told me that diabetes could be stopped naturally.

Of course I was skeptical… If it was true, why hadn't my doctor told me about it?

But after all, I had nothing to lose so I watched this video.

And I must say I was very surprised!

Inside, I learned that a Nigerian doctor, who is based in India, had found a natural and effective way to normalize blood sugar levels, using what he calls "The Diabetes Destroyer".

And his patients manage to get rid of their diabetes, in almost 100% of the cases.

So I followed the recommendations of this video, without much hope I must admit.

During the first days, I didn't see much change, but I decided to continue anyway.

I almost gave up after 3 weeks passed without any noticeable difference!

But I held on to a mustard seed sized faith and kept at it because many of his patients said they didn't have good results until the sixth week too.

Then, on the very fourth week, things started happening fast!

My blood sugar was down 69 points from 480 I recorded the previous day.

I didn't exercise at all but it still reduced anyway.

I know it sounds like magic to you just as it looked to me too that day.

Before the end of the week, I was recording a little bit over 200mg/dL.

The second month, I had a routine appointment with my doctor.

Well, he was "extremely surprised" (his words) by my blood sugar numbers. I was at 103 when I went to see him, at 9:30am in the morning.

Since then, I'm alive again.

The layers of fat below my belly were closing up at an alarming rate…

I could literally see the difference myself.

I was getting more active and I feel like I could get more things done during the day…

Every day I thank God, and especially my friend, for sending me this video."Want to know more?
